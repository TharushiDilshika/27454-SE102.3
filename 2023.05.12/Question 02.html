Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka is an island nation located in the Indian Ocean, just off the southeastern coast of India


Sri Lanka has a population of approximately 22 million people and is home to many cultures, languages, and ethnicities. The Sinhalese people form the majority of the nation's population, followed by the Tamils, who are the largest minority group and are concentrated in northern Sri Lanka;
both groups have played an influential role in the island's history.
Other long-established groups include the Moors, Indian Tamils, Burghers, Malays, Chinese, and Vedda
